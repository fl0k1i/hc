How it works
01
Reads mouth movement

The earset detects subtle jaw and mouth movements in real time using onboard sensors.

02
AI interprets intent

An onboard AI model translates those movements into intended speech, adapting to each patient's unique patterns.

03
Delivers air pressure

Dynamically modulated air is delivered directly into the oral cavity in real time.

04
Natural speech emerges

The patient's own mouth shapes the airflow into natural-sounding words — their voice, restored.
